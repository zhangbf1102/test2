Glossary of terms

The glossary of terms provides definitions for technical terms and abbreviations used in the user manual.

  • Ampere (A): The unit of measurement for electric current.

  • Connector Type: The type of connector used to connect the charging cable to the device.

  • DC (Direct Current): The type of electric current used to charge the battery of the device.

  • Input Voltage: The AC voltage range that the charger can accept.

  • Output Voltage: The DC voltage that the charger can provide.

  • Short Circuit: A condition where the current flows through a circuit without a load, causing damage to the device.

  • Type 2: The most common connector type used for EV-charging in Europe.
